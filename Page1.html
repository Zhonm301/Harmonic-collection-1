Exit
Even before my eyes opened, I'd felt this strange warmth
beaming on my face. I knew that I was lost.
I let out a sigh and let go of the rest
of the world. I was relieved that I was lost.
I touched the long, soft yellow grass and felt
the wind through my face and hair. I look down,
seeing this white dress on me for the first time.
And soon, I knew I would forget all of
what is left from the world I left behind.
As time passed, I felt a numbness in my brain,
as it prevented me from remembering-
slowly even losing my train of thoughts.
I no longer know the meaning of existance.
There is nothing left of me to know or do.
The only thing left is the cold wind and sun
hitting my skin as I sit here, on the grass
... This is my escape
